
📊 Food Delivery Data Analysis Dashboard (Talabat Case Study)
📝 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing the Talabat Enhanced Orders Dataset to drive data-driven decisions. The primary goal is to optimize operational performance, reduce delivery times, and maximize revenue by understanding the variables that impact the food delivery lifecycle
.
🎯 Key Objectives
Delivery Performance: Analyze factors affecting speed and efficiency
.
Customer Behavior: Identify popular products and preferred payment methods
.
Operational Efficiency: Provide actionable recommendations to improve business workflows
.
📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
The analysis reveals the following high-level business metrics:
Total Revenue: 26.89 Million EGP
.
Total Orders: 100,000 orders
.
Total Quantity Sold: 299,000 units
.
Average Order Value (AOV): 268.93 EGP
.
🔍 Deep Dive Insights
1. Sales & Geographical Analysis
Top Performing Cities: Sales are led by Zagazig (3.91M), followed closely by Tanta (3.90M), Cairo (3.86M), and Alexandria (3.84M)
.
Most Popular Items: Shawarma is the top-selling product (3.1M), followed by Pizza, Fried Chicken, Koshary, and Pasta (all averaging ~3.0M)
.
Payment Preferences: Customers are evenly split between Wallet (33.5%), Cash (33.26%), and Credit Card (33.24%), suggesting the need for a seamless multi-channel payment experience
.
2. Delivery & Logistics Performance
Vehicle Efficiency: Motorbikes have proven to be the most efficient delivery method, particularly in high-traffic scenarios
.
Traffic Impact: High traffic levels are a major bottleneck, significantly increasing delivery duration
.
Driver Availability: 90.08% of revenue is generated by "Online" drivers. Improving driver connectivity (minimizing "Offline" status) represents a potential growth opportunity worth 2.67M EGP in lost sales
.
💡 Strategic Recommendations
Based on the data insights, the following actions are recommended to boost performance:
Optimize Fleet: Increase the number of motorbikes to navigate traffic more effectively and reduce delivery times
.
Strategic Distribution: Reallocate drivers based on geographical demand, focusing on high-volume hubs like Zagazig and Tanta
.
Peak Hour Management: Adjust operations or offer incentives to motorbike riders during high-traffic periods to maintain service quality
.
Enhance Payment Systems: Continue supporting and optimizing all payment methods as they are equally utilized by the customer base
.
